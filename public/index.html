About MealWheel


MealWheel is a cheaper alternative to delivering food! With the rise in bicyclists, wouldn't it be convenient for food to be delivered at the same time? MealWheel is a way to link restaurants and cyclists. Restaurants part of this program will offer a cheaper alternative to delivering food utilizing cyclists. With MealWheel, cyclists can deliver the food while getting their exercise at the same time, and orderers can recieve their food with cheaper delivery prices. The best part is, cyclists don't need to set aside specific time! Whenever the cyclist wants to go out for a ride, they can alert restaurants nearby that they are free to cycle over and deliver. Not only is this convenient and cheap, we are also reducing carbon emissions and creating a healthier community!
